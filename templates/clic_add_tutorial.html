How to add a room's week list from CLiC:

  1. Log into CLiC, search for and navigate to "Venue Reservation Application"

  1. Click "Venue Calendar", type room name without pressing Enter. (If "Monthly" calendar displays events, it worked)
  2. Click "Week List". If schedule does not appear, repeat from step 3.
  3. Hold right click, drag over weekly schedule, and Copy
