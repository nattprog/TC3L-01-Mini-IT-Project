How to add a room's week list from CLiC:

  1. Log into CLiC and navigate to "Venue Reservation Application"
  2. Click "Venue Calendar" and type room name without pressing Enter (If the "Monthly" calendar displays events, it worked)
  3. Click "Week List" (If schedule does not appear, repeat from step 3)
  4. Hold right click, drag over weekly schedule, and Copy
  5. Paste and submit schedule on FCI Map!

If errors occur, refresh the page
